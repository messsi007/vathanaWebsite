HOW TO RUN A SUCCESSFUL REMOTE WORKSHOP

`

The shift to hybrid and remote work did not, in fact, destroy the art of collaboration. It actually brought us lots of new tools and opportunities to connect with our teams in a way that makes everyone more comfortable and ready to share.

In this webinar, we’ll share advice and on how to prep, facilitate, and synthesize a remote workshop that draws out the information and creates the alignment that you need. We’ll also keep the mic open for sharing and conversation about your specific needs.

Together, we’ll discuss:

  • Recommendations for tech tools and troubleshooting
  • Our approach to designing workshop exercises
  • How to keep people engaged and energized
  • How to synthesize and share back what you learned
